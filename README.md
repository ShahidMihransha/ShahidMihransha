## 🚀 Featured Project — TechVakkil

> AI-powered legal guidance and lawyer marketplace for India 🇮🇳

TechVakkil helps everyday Indian citizens understand the law in plain language, analyze legal documents, and connect with Bar Council-verified lawyers — with AI that runs **100% locally on-device**. No data ever leaves the user's network.

| Surface | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| 💬 **AI Legal Chat** | 5-type classifier — explains rights, procedures, urgent situations |
| 📄 **Document Analyzer** | 10-type classifier — analyzes contracts, FIRs, notices, court docs |
| ⚖️ **Lawyer Marketplace** | Bar Council-verified advocates, filterable, in-app messaging |
| 🏛️ **Law Library** | Searchable Indian acts with plain-language summaries |
| 🗄️ **Document Vault** | Per-user encrypted document storage with AI analysis history |
